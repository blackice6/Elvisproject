Help & Support

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt a dog from Paws & Homes?
To adopt, browse our available dogs on the home page, click "Adopt Me" on your favorite dog, and fill out our adoption application. Our team will review and contact you within 48 hours.
What health guarantees do you provide?
All our dogs come with full health certification, complete vaccinations, microchip, and a 30-day health guarantee. We also provide lifetime veterinary support for all our dogs.
What if I can't keep my dog?
We have an adoption return policy. If for any reason you cannot keep your dog, we will take them back with no questions asked. We want to ensure every dog finds a forever home.
Do you offer training services?
Yes! All our dogs receive professional training before adoption. We also offer continued training support and can provide recommendations for trainers in your area.
Are your dogs socialized?
Absolutely. All our dogs undergo extensive socialization with other dogs, people, and different environments to ensure they are well-adjusted and friendly.
What is your shipping policy?
We offer safe, professional delivery services to most locations. Puppies are shipped in temperature-controlled environments with proper care and monitoring throughout the journey.
Do you deliver within Kenya?
Yes, we deliver throughout Kenya. We have partnerships with safe, reliable delivery services to ensure your new companion arrives safely to your home.
How much does a dog cost?
Our prices range from KES 15,000 to KES 35,000 depending on breed, age, and quality. Featured puppies and special offers are available at competitive prices throughout the year.
Can I meet the dogs before adoption?
Yes! We encourage potential families to visit our facility in Nairobi. We're open 9AM-6PM daily. Please call ahead to schedule a viewing.
What support do you provide after adoption?
We provide lifetime support including free veterinary advice, training consultations, and behavioral support. Our team is available 24/7 for any questions or concerns.
